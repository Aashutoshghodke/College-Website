Overview

Terna Public Charitable Trusts College,Osmanabad is a private engineering institute established in 1989 with the affiliation of DBATU, Lonere and recognition of AICTE & DTE. TPCTs COE offers UG and PG courses in various specializations. The college has an Industry-oriented curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced & qualified faculty, and more. It facilitates innovation-driven and multifaceted technical learning among the students, which helps them evolve into proficient. There are over 70 faculty members in the college out of which 99% faculty members are postgraduates whereas 12 faculty members are pursuing PhD. All the Faculty members at TPCTs COE are dedicated and very hardworking.
